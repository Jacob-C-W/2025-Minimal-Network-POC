# NextDNS

NextDNS is a cloud hosted DNS platform that is entirely free, however its advanced features are limited to 300,000 queries a month. Once the amount is exceeded, it performs as a PDNS without the extra features. 
#
It handles blocklist, allowing manual configuration or subscription to managed feeds that provide curated URL Filtering. 
#
It tracks all DNS traffic, blocks, permits, and with some setup allows tracking of specific device DNS queries. It has a map of queries and where they originate. It has summaries to explain popular addresses-what they are, what they do, and how much they're known to track.  
#
Overall it's an intensely easy and robust featureset with no cost and can be scaled beyond a few users and dozen devices with some investment into their subscription model.
