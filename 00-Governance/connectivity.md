# Connectivity

Common guidelines:

- Internet access must support common small-business needs [WAN]:
    - cloud applications
    - vendor portals 
    - email
    - collaboration
    - software updates

- Internet must support local small-business needs [LAN]:
    - local services
    - printers 
    - internal business

- Wi-Fi must be provided as a required access method to support business mobility and modern device use in a small office environment.
    - BYOD
    - Business Machines
    - Internet of Things
#
    Local connectivity must support growth and day-to-day change (new devices, onboarding, and basic troubleshooting) without requiring redesign for routine changes.
#

• The network must provide consistent DNS behavior suitable for business use and troubleshooting (DNS is a critical dependency for “internet access”).

• The network should support basic traffic prioritization or bandwidth controls to protect business operations during congestion events.

• Connectivity health must be measurable and reviewable by leadership

#

SLA of four nines (99.99%) is a common requirement. 

**SLA: 99.99%:**

    Daily: 8.6s
    Weekly: 1m 0.48s
    Monthly: 4m 23s
    Quarterly: 13m 8.9s
    Yearly: 52m 36s
