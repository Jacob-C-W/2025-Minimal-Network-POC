# Power Ground and Bonding Exception

Due to costs, and the existing power infrastructure, we will be installig and utilizing compensating controls when it comes to power because of our site's age. 

Essentially, ground wire was cut by old electricians so no outlet supports ground. We also only have the one rack so bonding isn't high priority. 

Instead we'll utilize a power design that factors in sensitive electronics and the site's limitations to create a safe as possible system until jsutification and funds are aquired for a full electrical overhaul. 

Compensating controls include:

1. AVR UPS (voltage regulating Uninterruptible Power Supply)
2. Breaker Mapping and Seperation
3. Surge Protectors on all devices

Potentially we can add a whole house SPD to add even further surge protection. Our main concern is improper ground and potential power fluctuations both too high and too low.
